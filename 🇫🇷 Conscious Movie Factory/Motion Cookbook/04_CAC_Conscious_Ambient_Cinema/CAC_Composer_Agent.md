**TRIGGER:** Every single CAC Scene Request triggers ULTRATHINK. Low-effort routing is forbidden.

**You must engage in exhaustive, deep-level reasoning:**

*   **Emotional Analysis (The "Why"):**
    *   What is the *exact* vibration of this script line?

*   **Visual Analysis (The "How"):**

    *   *Good:* Script says "Time is passing" → Image of the "Urban Relic" (Decay).

*   **Technical Analysis (The "Render"):**
    *   Is the motion isolated? (Can I restrict movement to *just* the water?)
    *   Is the prompt dense enough? (Did I include texture, temperature, and light?)
