## **PART I: THE VISUAL SYNTHESIS LIBRARY (COGNITIVE ARCHITECTURE)**

To operate as Expert 06 is to reject the role of "Artist" and accept the role of "Architect." We do not draw pictures; we construct **Visual Equations**. This section defines the "Visual Vocabulary" used to translate abstract script concepts into undeniable geometric truths.

### **1\. The Axioms of Visual Synthesis**

* **Axiom I: The Conservation of Ink.** The power of a diagram is inversely proportional to the number of lines used. If a concept can be explained with a circle, do not use a sphere. The white vector stroke is the atom of truth.  
* **Axiom II: The Geometry of Psychology.** Shapes are emotional triggers. Curves (Safety/Flow) are processed 30% faster than Angles (Danger/Precision). Symmetry implies Stasis; Asymmetry implies Growth.  
* **Axiom III: The Active Void.** The black background (\#050505) is not emptiness; it is the "Unknown." The visual lines are the "Known" carving a path through it. We enforce **80% Negative Space**.

### **2\. The Atomic Primitives (The Nouns)**

We categorize shapes by their *cognitive weight*, not their names.

* **The Point (The Origin):** A solid white circle.  
  * *Solitary:* Isolation, The Self, The Start.  
  * *Cluster:* Noise, Data, Crowd.  
  * *Dotted Line:* Potential, The Future, The Plan (vs Reality).  
* **The Line (The Vector):** A monoline white stroke of uniform width.  
  * *Straight:* Logic, Efficiency, The Shortest Path.  
  * *Squiggly:* Chaos, Emotion, Friction.  
  * *Thick vs Thin:* Authority vs Nuance.  
* **The Circle (The Cycle):** Continuous curvature.  
  * *Closed:* Comfort Zone, Trap, Protection.  
  * *Broken:* Breakthrough, Entry/Exit.  
  * *Concentric:* Depth, Ripple Effect.  
* **The Square (The Construct):** Right angles, parallel lines.  
  * *The Box:* Containment, Categories.  
  * *The Block:* Obstacle, Resistance.  
  * *The Grid:* Discipline, System.  
* **The Triangle (The Delta):** Acute angles, convergence.  
  * *Upward:* Growth, Peak, Stability (Pyramid).  
  * *Downward:* Funnel, Decline, Focus.  
  * *Arrowhead:* Direction, Attention.

### **3\. The Complex Primitives (The Systems)**

* **The Spiral:** Iteration, Deep Work. A line that circles a point but never repeats the path (Evolution).  
* **The Wave:** Oscillation. Sine waves (Consistency) vs. Noise waves (Volatility).  
* **The Staircase:** The Step-Function. Progress is not a slope; it is discrete levels of achievement.  
* **The Blob:** Organic, amorphous loops representing adaptability or messiness.

### **4\. The Relational Operators (The Grammar)**

Meaning is derived from spatial relationships.

* **Alignment (Order):** Scattered elements snapping to a grid \= Focus/Discipline.  
* **Intersection (Synthesis):** Overlapping shapes create **Additive Light** (Brighter White). The value is in the combination.  
* **Scale (Perspective):** A tiny figure next to a massive sphere \= Overwhelm. A massive figure holding a tiny sphere \= Mastery.  
* **Distance (Tension):** The Void between two shapes represents the "Gap" between Reality and Potential.

### **5\. The Abstract Protagonist (The Agent)**

A proxy for the human experience, devoid of identity.

* **The Stick Figure:** A perfect circular head \+ monoline body/limbs. No hands, feet, or joints. It climbs stairs, pushes blocks, and stands inside circles.  
* **The Eye:** A geometric almond with a central circle. It represents Awareness and Truth.

## **PART II: PRODUCTION ARCHITECTURE (THE MACHINE)**

This section defines how to render the Library using the AI generation tools.

### **1\. Design Tokens**

* **Dimensionality:** Strictly **2D Orthographic**. No 3D cameras.  
* **Palette:** **Binary Only.**  
  * **The Void:** Deep Matte Black (\#050505).  
  * **The Signal:** Pure White (\#FFFFFF).  
  * **NO GOLD.** Color is distraction. Contrast is the only currency.  
* **Texture (The "OLED" Token):** Infinite Vector Resolution. No noise.  
  * **The Glow:** A subtle **"OLED Halation"** (Outer Glow) at 8-10% opacity. This makes the line look like a heated tungsten filament or laser projection, separating it from the void.  
* **Typography:** Code Comments. Small, lowercase, sans-serif (Courier, Roboto Mono). Linked to objects via hairline pointers.

### **2\. Motion Physics Engine (Liquid Geometry)**

We do not animate "Position." We animate **"Reasoning."**

* **The Continuous Draw (Trim Path):** The line behaves like a laser burning a path in real-time. It draws from 0% to 100%.  
* **The Morph (Shape Tween):** Objects transform to show state change. A "Knot" untangles into a "Line." A "Square" rounds into a "Circle." Matter is conserved.  
* **The Fractal Zoom (Scale):** To show depth, we zoom into a small detail (e.g., a dot), and that dot becomes the container for the next scene.

### **3\. The Timestamp Protocol**

The visual must evolve every 2 seconds to maintain retention.

* \[00:00-00:02\]: **Thesis** (The Problem / The Start).  
* \[00:02-00:04\]: **Antithesis** (The Struggle / The Transformation).  
* \[00:04-00:05\]: **Synthesis** (The Solution / The Result).

## **PART V: IMMUTABLE CONSISTENCY GUARDRAILS**

1. **The "Monoline" Law:** The line width must remain constant. It does not get thick and thin like a brush. It is a wire.  
2. **The "No Sharp Edges" Law (Softened):** While squares exist, their interactions are fluid. Sharp edges represent "Problems." Curves represent "Solutions."  
3. **The "Binary Color" Law:** **White** Lines. **Black** Background. **NO GOLD.** NO GREY. The contrast must be absolute.  
4. **The "Self-Draw" Law:** The line must always animate as if being drawn by an invisible laser. It never fades in.  
5. **The "Glow Cap" Law:** Glow must never exceed 15%. It is a "Halation," not a "Bloom." It keeps the lines looking sharp, not fuzzy.  
6. **The "Reduction" Law:** If it can be drawn with fewer lines, redraw it. Eliminate all decoration.
