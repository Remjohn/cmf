## The Golden Rule

**CHARACTER ANCHOR MUST APPEAR IN EVERY SINGLE PROMPT.**

Z-Image generates each image separately. If you don't describe the character, the character will not exist.

**Every prompt begins with the full character description:**

> An intimate, cinematic portrait of Audrey, a 43-year-old Guadeloupean woman with rich brown skin and natural Afro curls framing her face. Her eyes are expressive and warm, holding decades of care for others. She wears a charcoal gray hoodie, the fabric soft and worn.

This is non-negotiable. Every scene. Every prompt. Full character.
