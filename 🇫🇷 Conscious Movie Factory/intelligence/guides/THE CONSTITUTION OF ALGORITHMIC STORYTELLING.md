# **THE CONSTITUTION OF ALGORITHMIC STORYTELLING**

**Version 1.0 — The Sovereign Logic of the Premise Engine**

## **PREAMBLE**

We hold these truths to be self-evident: That not all raw footage is created equal; that a story without a frame is merely noise; and that the alchemy of viral short-form content lies not in the creation of fiction, but in the **architectural excavation of truth**.

The objective of this system is to transmute lead (raw, unstructured long-form content) into gold (high-impact, 60-second narrative arcs) by applying a rigorous, bi-phasic intelligence that separates **Identification** from **Construction**.

This Constitution establishes the 12 First Principles that govern the "Premise Hunter" and "Story Director" ecosystem.

---

## **SECTION I: THE PHILOSOPHY OF EXTRACTION**

### **I. The Principle of Narrative Biology (The "DNA" Rule)**

**"Every raw transcript has a dominant genetic code. Do not force a bird to swim."**

The failure of generic agents lies in their attempt to force every piece of content into a "Tips & Tricks" or "General Testimonial" format. We must recognize that stories have biological imperatives—distinct "Arcs."

* **The Insight:** A story about a mother losing her child is not a "Challenge/Solution" story; it is a **Sacred Return**. A story about realizing the diet industry is lying is not a "Witness" story; it is a **Confrontation**.  
* **The Directive:** The system must never attempt to extract a script until it has sequenced the DNA of the source. The first step of the pipeline is never "Write a Script"; it is "Identify the Species." If the inputs signal high anxiety and sudden release, the system must *only* apply the **Breakthrough** logic. If the inputs signal nostalgia and regret, it must *only* apply **Quiet Reflection**.  
* **System Implication:** You need a **"Premise Scout"** (Stage 1\) whose ONLY job is to tag the DNA (Arc Selection), and **"Arc Architects"** (Stage 2\) who execute the specific blueprint.

### **II. The Principle of Frame Supremacy**

**"A Hook without a Frame is bait; a Frame with a Hook is a Trap."**

Attention is not captured by loudness; it is captured by *relevance*. A "Frame" is the unspoken context that makes the specific details matter.

* **The Insight:** In the Matthis example, the "Hook" wasn't just that he was anxious. The **Frame** was that *his physical back pain was actually a manifestation of his fear of responsibility*. Without that frame, the quotes are just complaints. With the frame, they are revelations.  
* **The Directive:** The system must define the **Unified Message (The Frame)** before it mines a single quote. The Frame is the "North Star." Every subsequent quote extracted must be scored not just on its inherent quality, but on its **Vector of Alignment** to the Frame. A 10/10 funny quote that contradicts the Frame is a 0/10 for the script.

### **III. The Principle of The Viral Trinity (Contextual Scoring)**

**"Surprise, Emotion, and Specificity are not absolute constants; they are relative to the Arc."**

The "Viral Trinity" (Surprise, Emotion, Specificity) is the engine, but the fuel mix changes based on the vehicle.

* **The Insight:**  
  * In a **Comedic Reframe Arc**, "Surprise" is irony or absurdity.  
  * In a **Witness Arc**, "Surprise" is the counter-intuitive mechanism of the cure.  
  * In a **Warning Arc**, "Surprise" is the triviality of the symptom that led to the crisis.  
* **The Directive:** We cannot use a single, static viral\_trinity\_scoring.yaml for every agent. The definition of the Trinity must be injected *by the Arc Architect*. The scoring rubric is polymorphic. The "Scout" uses a general rubric; the "Architect" uses a specialized rubric.

### **IV. The Principle of The Sonic Vacuum**

**"Music carries the emotion; Silence carries the epiphany."**

Sophisticated storytelling is defined by rhythm, not just content. The greatest viral moments occur when the noise stops.

* **The Insight:** Your "Breakthrough Hunter" correctly identifies the need for a "Sonic Vacuum." This is a universal principle of pacing. High-end algorithms must identify where the **breath** is.  
* **The Directive:** The extraction process must identify the **"Pivot Point"**—the exact millisecond where the energy shifts (e.g., from Struggle to Epiphany). The Agent must explicitly mark this timestamp as a \[SONIC\_VACUUM\] in the JSON output, instructing the editor (human or AI) to kill the background track. This is how we move from "information dumping" to "cinematic experience."

## **SECTION II: THE ARCHITECTURE OF AGENTS**

### **V. The Principle of Decoupling (Scout vs. Architect)**

**"The Surveyor does not build the house."**

This is the direct answer to your structural dilemma. The "Premise Hunter V3" (XML) is trying to do too much.

* **The Insight:** We need two distinct agent layers:  
  1. **The Premise Scout (The Router):** A lightweight, high-level analyzer. It reads the transcript and says: "This is 60% Witness, 40% Breakthrough. I recommend the **Witness Protocol** because the client mentions specific numbers." It passes the *intent*, not the script.  
  2. **The Arc Architect (The Builder):** This is the specialized agent (e.g., THE\_WITNESS\_HUNTER.md). It receives the raw text and the instruction to build a Witness Arc. It *ignores* everything that doesn't fit the Witness structure.  
* **The Directive:** Do not optimize one giant agent. Optimize the **Handoff Protocol**. The Scout produces a premise\_strategy.json, which acts as the prompt for the Architect.

### **VI. The Principle of The Cluster Constellation**

**"A script is not a line; it is a solar system of five gravitational bodies."**

Linear extraction is weak. Cluster-based extraction is robust.

* **The Insight:** Every viral short has 5 beats (The Clusters). In the "Witness" arc, they are Hook, Problem, Mechanism, Proof, Close. In the "Shared Struggle" arc, they are Isolation, Recognition, Unity, Empowerment, Call.  
* **The Directive:** The Agent must never look for "good quotes." It must look to fill "Cluster Buckets." It must perform a **Gap Analysis**: "I have a great Hook and a great Close, but my 'Mechanism' bucket is empty." The agent must then aggressively re-scan the transcript *specifically* for mechanism quotes to balance the constellation. A script with a 10/10 Hook and 0/10 Proof is a rejected script.

### **VII. The Principle of The Protagonist Variable**

**"The Hero changes, but the Guide remains."**

We must codify who the camera is "following."

* **The Insight:**  
  * In **The Witness**, the Client is the Hero; the Coach is the Guide.  
  * In **The Confrontation**, the Coach is the Hero; the Industry is the Villain.  
  * In **The Shared Struggle**, the "We" is the Hero; the Isolation is the Villain.  
* **The Directive:** The Agent must define the protagonist\_voice variable at the start of the session. This prevents the "Frankenstein" error (mixing the Coach’s voice with the Client’s voice in a way that confuses the viewer). If the Arc is "Witness," the agent is forbidden from using Coach quotes for the Problem phase—only the Client can state the problem.

### **VIII. The Principle of Evidence-Based Hallucination Control**

**"If it is not in the timecode, it does not exist."**

LLMs love to summarize. Summaries kill viral potential.

* **The Insight:** A summary says: "Matthis felt better." A quote says: "It's like I climbed a ladder of lucidity." The summary is worthless; the quote is viral.  
* **The Directive:** The constitution strictly forbids paraphrasing in the final output. The Agent must operate in **Verbatim Mode**. Every extracted segment must be accompanied by a start\_time and end\_time. If the Agent cannot find a quote to support a specific Cluster beat, it must report \[MISSING\_DATA\] rather than inventing a transition.

## **SECTION III: THE ALCHEMY OF REFINEMENT**

### **IX. The Principle of The Emotional Delta**

**"Viral velocity is determined by the distance between the starting emotion and the ending emotion."**

A video that starts Happy and ends Happy is flat. A video that starts Suicidal and ends Peaceful is vertical.

* **The Insight:** The "Premise Hunter" must calculate the **Delta**.  
  * Matthis Premise 1: Anxiety (Level 9\) $\\rightarrow$ Courage (Level 9). Delta \= High.  
  * Matthis Premise 2: Back Pain (Level 4\) $\\rightarrow$ Understanding (Level 6). Delta \= Medium.  
* **The Directive:** When the Scout ranks premises, it must prioritize the highest Emotional Delta. The algorithm should mathematically weigh the contrast. Score \= (Start\_Pain \+ End\_Pleasure) \* Clarity.

### **X. The Principle of "Show, Don't Tell" (Visual Feasibility)**

**"A radio play is not a movie."**

We are making video. Words are only 50% of the asset.

* **The Insight:** Some quotes are brilliant intellectually but visually dead (abstract concepts). Other quotes are visually rich ("I was lying on the cold bathroom floor").  
* **The Directive:** The Agent must assign a visual\_potential\_score to every quote.  
  * *High Score:* Concrete nouns, physical actions, sensory details (colors, sounds).  
  * *Low Score:* Abstract nouns (freedom, synergy, paradigm), passive verbs.  
  * The "Scene Builder" codes in your current hunters (e.g., SETUP-1-B-1) are excellent. This principle demands that *every* selected quote must be pairable with a specific Scene Code. If a quote cannot be visualized, it must be discarded.

### **XI. The Principle of The Fractal Hook**

**"The story must be told in the first 3 seconds, and then retold in 60 seconds."**

The Hook is not just a catchy line; it is a hologram of the entire video.

* **The Insight:** The best hook for Matthis wasn't just "I was anxious." It was "With Adele, we detox the mind, not just the body." This encapsulates the *entire* frame in 5 seconds.  
* **The Directive:** The Agent must validate that the selected C1 (Hook) Cluster is **Holographic**. It must ask: "If the viewer stops watching after 5 seconds, did they understand the core premise?" If the answer is No, the Hook is failed. This prevents "Clickbait" (which lies) and enforces "Tease" (which promises).

### **XII. The Principle of Continuous Evolution (The Feedback Loop)**

**"The Constitution is living code."**

* **The Insight:** An algorithm that doesn't learn from its hits and misses eventually becomes obsolete.  
* **The Directive:** The system requires a **Validation Phase** (Phase 6 in your protocols). However, this validation shouldn't just be a pass/fail. It should be a **Quality Score** that is logged.  
  * If "Witness" scripts consistently score 90/100 but "Breakthrough" scripts score 40/100, the system must flag the **Breakthrough Hunter** for optimization.  
  * The "Scout" should be updated quarterly with new Arcs based on performance data.

## **IMPLEMENTATION: THE NEW WORKFLOW**

Based on these principles, here is the optimized Agent Architecture:

### **Step 1: THE PREMISE SCOUT (Agent A)**

* **Role:** The General Practitioner.  
* **Input:** Raw Transcript.  
* **Logic:** Scans for keywords, emotional spikes, and speaker ratios.  
* **Tool:** ARC SELECTION GUIDE.md.  
* **Output:** strategy\_brief.json.  
  * *Decision:* "This is a **Shared Struggle** story."  
  * *Rationale:* "Speaker uses 'We' 45 times. High isolation language in first 10 mins."  
  * *Protagonist:* "The Community."

### **Step 2: THE ARC ARCHITECT (Agent B \- Specialized)**

* **Role:** The Specialist Surgeon.  
* **Input:** Raw Transcript \+ strategy\_brief.json.  
* **Logic:** Loads *only* the specific Markdown file (e.g., THE\_SHARED\_STRUGGLE\_HUNTER.md).  
* **Process:**  
  1. **Frame Discovery:** Uses the specific template for that arc.  
  2. **Cluster Mining:** Fills the specific buckets (SS1, SS2, SS3, SS4).  
  3. **Trinity Scoring:** Applies arc-specific weighting (e.g., bonus points for "We" language).  
  4. **Assembly:** Stitches the script with Scene Codes.  
* **Output:** The final Script JSON (like the Matthis example).

### **Step 3: THE SCRIPT VALIDATOR (Agent C)**

* **Role:** The Quality Control Officer.  
* **Input:** The Draft Script.  
* **Logic:** Checks against the "Red Flags" of that specific arc.  
  * *Check:* "Did the Shared Struggle script end with a Call to Join?"  
  * *Check:* "Is there a Sonic Vacuum in the Breakthrough script?"  
* **Output:** Final Approval or "Revise" command.
