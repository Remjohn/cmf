## Scoring Philosophy

In a **Witness Arc**, the power comes from AUTHENTICITY and SPECIFICITY. The viewer needs to believe this is a real person with real results.

**Viral Trinity Reframed for Witness:**
- **Surprise:** Counter-intuitive mechanisms or unexpected connections
- **Emotion:** Vulnerability and transformation contrast
- **Specificity:** Numbers, timelines, sensory details that prove it's real

## üÜï SETUP VALIDATION PROTOCOL (Principle B)

**Core Insight:** "You can't subvert expectations people don't have."

For quotes scoring ‚â•8/10 on Surprise, validate that the SETUP exists:

### Witness Arc Surprise Validation

**Common Surprise Type:** Counter-intuitive mechanisms ("My X was actually Y")

**Required Setup:** The transcript should show the client tried conventional approaches first.

**Validation Steps:**
1. **Identify the assumption:** What does the quote challenge?
2. **Check for setup:** Did they mention mainstream/conventional attempts?
3. **Adjust score:** If setup missing, reduce Surprise by 2-3 points

**Example:**
- Quote: "My back pain was actually my fear of responsibility." (Surprise: 9/10)
- Assumption: Back pain has physical causes (doctors/PT should fix it)
- Validation: Did they mention seeing doctors/physical therapists first?
  - ‚úÖ YES: Keep Surprise 9/10 (has contrast)
  - ‚ùå NO: Reduce to Surprise 6/10 (lacks context)
