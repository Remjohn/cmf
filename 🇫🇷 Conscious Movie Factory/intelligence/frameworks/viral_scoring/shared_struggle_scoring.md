## Scoring Philosophy

In a **Shared Struggle Arc**, the power comes from COLLECTIVE VALIDATION. "I thought I was alone, but we're all fighting this."

**Viral Trinity Reframed:**
- **Surprise:** The realization that this struggle is UNIVERSAL, not personal failure
- **Emotion:** Loneliness ‚Üí Belonging. "You're not broken. We ALL feel this."
- **Specificity:** "We" language. Naming the shared enemy. Community size/reach.

## üÜï SETUP VALIDATION PROTOCOL (Principle B)

**Core Insight:** Universality is only surprising if isolation was established first.

### Shared Struggle Arc Surprise Validation

**Common Surprise Type:** "This is UNIVERSAL, not just me"

**Required Setup:** Transcript should show isolation/shame ("I thought I was alone")

**Validation Steps:**
1. **Check for isolation:** Did they express feeling alone/defective earlier?
2. **Adjust score:** If isolation not established, reduce Surprise by 2-3 points

**Example:**
- Quote: "90% of women feel this. You're not broken." (Surprise: 9/10)
- Validation: Did they say "I thought I was the only one" or similar earlier?
  - ‚úÖ YES: Keep Surprise 9/10 (validates isolation ‚Üí universal shift)
  - ‚ùå NO: Reduce to Surprise 6/10 (stat without personal context)
