## Scoring Philosophy

In a **Breakthrough Arc**, the power comes from the SUDDEN SHIFT. The "Wait..." moment. The realization that changes everything.

**Viral Trinity Reframed for Breakthrough:**
- **Surprise:** The revelation itself. The pattern interrupt. "I thought X, but actually Y."
- **Emotion:** The contrast between BEFORE (anxiety, suffocation) and AFTER (clarity, breath)
- **Specificity:** The exact moment it clicked. The phrase that triggered it. The body sensation.

Principle B)

**Core Insight:** Binary flips require the "I thought X" setup.

### Breakthrough Arc Surprise Validation

**Common Surprise Type:** Binary flip ("I thought X, but actually NOT X")

**Required Setup:** The "I thought X" part must exist in transcript BEFORE the flip.

**Validation Steps:**
1. **Identify the flip:** What assumption is being reversed?
2. **Check for setup:** Did they express "I thought X" earlier in transcript?
3. **Adjust score:** If setup missing, reduce Surprise by 3 points

**Example:**
- Quote: "Letting go WAS the control." (Surprise: 10/10)
- Flip: Control ‚â† controlling; control = letting go
- Validation: Did they say "I thought I needed to control everything" earlier?
  - ‚úÖ YES: Keep Surprise 10/10 (perfect flip)
