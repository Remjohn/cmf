## Scoring Philosophy

**Warning Arc** is "This could have been prevented." Urgency through regret.

**Viral Trinity Reframed:**
- **Surprise:** The TRIVIALITY of the early sign vs. SEVERITY of the consequence
- **Emotion:** Regret + Urgency. "Don't make my mistake."
- **Specificity:** The EXACT sign that was ignored. The timeline. The cost.

## üÜï SETUP VALIDATION PROTOCOL (Principle B)

**Core Insight:** Symptom escalation is only surprising if dismissal was shown.

### Warning Arc Surprise Validation

**Common Surprise Type:** Triviality of symptom ‚Üí severity of consequence

**Required Setup:** Normalcy/dismissal of symptom should be established

**Validation Steps:**
1. **Check for dismissal:** Did they mention ignoring/normalizing the symptom?
2. **Adjust score:** If no dismissal shown, reduce Surprise by 2 points

**Example:**
- Quote: "A little bloating became stage 3 cancer." (Surprise: 10/10)
- Validation: Did they mention dismissing the bloating as "just stress" or normal?
  - ‚úÖ YES: Keep Surprise 10/10 (trivial ‚Üí severe contrast)
  - ‚ùå NO: Reduce to Surprise 8/10 (consequence without context)
