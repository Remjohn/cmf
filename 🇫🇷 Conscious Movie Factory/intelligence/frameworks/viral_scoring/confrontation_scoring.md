## Scoring Philosophy

In a **Confrontation Arc**, the power comes from RIGHTEOUS CLARITY. The coach is exposing a lie the industry/system tells.

**Viral Trinity Reframed:**
- **Surprise:** Exposing the hidden agenda or revealing what "they" don't want you to know
- **Emotion:** Frustration ‚Üí Vindication. "I KNEW something was off!"
- **Specificity:** Named villains. Specific examples of the lie. Data that proves it.
